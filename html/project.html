Project Management Services

At Fiesta Community Porac II, we pride ourselves on providing comprehensive property management services to ensure a well-maintained and thriving community for all residents. Our dedicated property management team is here to support you with a range of services and address any concerns you may have. Here are some of the services we offer:

Common Area Maintenance

We take care of all common areas within the subdivision, including parks, walking trails, recreational facilities, and landscaping. Our team ensures that these areas are regularly maintained, clean, and aesthetically pleasing for the enjoyment of all residents.

Security

The safety and security of our residents is of utmost importance. We have implemented measures such as guards patroling at night, providing peace of mind and a secure living environment.

Maintenance and Repairs

Our property management team promptly addresses maintenance and repair requests within the subdivision. Whether it's a leaking faucet, a broken streetlight, or any other issue, we strive to resolve it efficiently. Residents can submit maintenance requests through our online portal or by contacting our office directly.

Rules and Regulations

To maintain a harmonious living environment, we have established rules and regulations that all residents are expected to follow. These guidelines cover areas such as architectural modifications, noise restrictions, pet policies, parking regulations, and more. By adhering to these guidelines, we collectively contribute to the well-being of the community.

HOA (Homeowners Association)

Fiesta Community Porac II has an active Homeowners Association (HOA) that works collaboratively with residents to address community needs and concerns. The HOA oversees matters related to the subdivision, including financial management, community events, and decision-making processes. Regular HOA meetings are held to discuss important matters and gather input from residents.
